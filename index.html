North Star Azure Group

A professional and welcoming Azure Fundamentals user group for learners, technology professionals, students, and community members who want a clear place to build cloud knowledge, stay informed, and connect through thoughtful bi-monthly meetings.

Register Contact Us

Group Description

North Star Azure Group is focused on making Azure Fundamentals more accessible through a clean, practical, and community-driven learning experience. The group is designed for people who are beginning their cloud journey as well as those who want to strengthen their understanding of core Azure concepts.

Each gathering is intended to provide concise learning, relevant Microsoft Azure updates, and meaningful discussion in a format that respects people’s time while building a strong local network of support and shared growth.
